## Why I Started This Blog

People sometimes ask how I balance the military stuff with tech stuff. Honestly? They're not that different. Both involve figuring things out, adapting when things go wrong, and trying to stay ahead of problems.

I started this blog because I wanted somewhere to write about what I'm working on and thinking about. Maybe I'll review some gear I actually use, talk about coding projects I'm building in my free time, or just share random thoughts about life. We'll see where it goes.
